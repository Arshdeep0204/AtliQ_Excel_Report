# AtliQ_Excel_Report

## Project Objectives

This project focuses on building structured and insightful reports for AtliQ by transforming raw business data into meaningful financial and operational insights. The project is centered around generating sales and financial reports that support decision-making, benchmarking, and performance tracking across various business dimensions.

---

## Sales Report

### 1. Customer Performance Report  
- Conducted an in-depth analysis of customer performance over the years.  
- Identified growth patterns, sales trends, and customer-wise revenue contributions to highlight high-performing and underperforming segments.

### 2. Market Performance and Sales Targets Comparison  
- Developed a comprehensive report comparing actual market performance with sales targets.  
- Provided clear visual insights to evaluate achievement levels, identify gaps, and support strategic planning.

---

## Finance Report

### 1. Profit and Loss (P&L) Reports by Fiscal Year and Months  
- Generated detailed P&L analysis broken down by fiscal year and individual months.  
- Helped evaluate financial performance trends, profitability, and cost structure across time periods.

### 2. Profit and Loss (P&L) Reports by Markets  
- Created market-wise P&L reports to benchmark financial performance across business regions.  
- Delivered insights into regional revenue, expenses, and profit margins, supporting market-focused strategy refinement.

## Technical Skills

- **Data Analysis Techniques:** Applied ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) frameworks to clean, transform, and prepare data efficiently.

- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Used DAX to create advanced calculated measures and dynamic KPIs for financial evaluation.
